Our Story

Crafting Art with
Heart & Soul

Pooja Frame Maker & Arts is a creative studio rooted in Pune, born from a deep passion for turning ordinary moments into extraordinary keepsakes. Founded by Manik and Prem Agwane, we specialize in handcrafted photo frames, custom pencil & portrait sketches, elegant marriage name plates, expressive wall paintings, and beautifully detailed board art — all crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a personal touch that machines simply can't replicate.

  • Handcrafted with premium materials
  • Personalized designs for every occasion
  • Expert repair & restoration services
  • Serving Pune & surroundings for 10+ years
  • 500+ delighted customers & counting

Our Services

From heartfelt gifting to grand wall installations — we bring your vision to life with craftsmanship you can feel.

Photo Frames & Gifts

Custom-sized photo frames in wood, metal & acrylic finishes. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries & corporate gifting.

Lighting Photo & Repairing

LED-lit photo displays & frame restoration. We breathe new life into damaged or faded frames with expert repair work.
