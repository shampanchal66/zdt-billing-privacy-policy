Privacy Policy — ZDT Billing

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ZDT Billing stores all data locally on your device only. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any external server. The app functions 100% offline.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Product inventory: name, price, stock, category, HSN code
  • Bills and invoices: customer name, items, amounts, dates, tax details
  • Subscription status: plan type (Basic/Monthly/Yearly), expiry date
  • App preferences: theme, currency, default tax rate

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or address
  • We do NOT track your location or movements
  • We do NOT access your contacts, call logs, SMS, or camera
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services

4. Google Sign-In (Optional)

Google Sign-In is available for login convenience. When you sign in with Google:

  • We only access your basic profile (name and email) for display purposes
  • Your Google credentials are managed by Google's authentication system
  • We do NOT store your Google password
  • You can disconnect Google Sign-In in app settings

5. Advertisements (Free Version Only)

The free version displays banner ads via Google AdMob:

  • No personal data is shared with advertisers
  • Ads are shown based on app context only
  • Premium subscribers (paid plans) enjoy an ad-free experience

6. Data Control & Deletion

  • Export data: Use "Export to Excel" in Settings
  • Delete all data: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

7. Data Security

All data is stored locally using SQLite. Regular Excel exports are recommended.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Effective Date" will be updated for changes.
